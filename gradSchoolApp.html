Applying to Grad School

Applications for graduate school usually begin towards the winter of the year you intend to graduate. However, this will all depend on the school you decide to apply to, and what their application deadlines are! As this process will vary based on this fact, it is important that you look up the specific school you are interested in. You will be able to find their graduate application information online on their website.

Some specific information that might be required of you is a transcript from your undergrad, as well as letters of reference. You can think about professors or other individuals who you might want to write these letters for you. They should know you well and be able to speak positively about your character and work ability.
