Benefits of WISc for Grad School

As part of the WISc program, there are some inherent advantages that will help you when it comes time to apply to graduate school, if that is something you’re interested in.

For one, being in WISc means that you will often work in small class sizes with some of the same professors, over an extended period of time. They will get to know you well, and as such, can be excellent references for grad school applications.

Another benefit of WISc is that the 4th year thesis course is required. This project is highly intensive and requires lots of independent time regulation. The entire course is excellent practice for grad school, which is also largely independent project-based on a particular area of research.
