Leadership Opportunities in WISc

As part of WISc, there are certain opportunities that come up over the years that you might like to participate in.

One of these opportunities is the Ontario Universities’ Fair (OUF). Usually, WISc likes to send a couple of students to this event to talk about the program and get new students interested in applying! An email will go out to all WISc students before the Fair every year, and you will be able to reply if you are interested in representing the program.

Another opportunity that all upper-year students can get involved in is mentoring for younger WISc students. Once you are in second year, an email will generally be sent out during the year asking if you would like to be a mentor to younger students. This will generally involve being put in contact with a small group of students, who will be able to ask you for advice when needed.
