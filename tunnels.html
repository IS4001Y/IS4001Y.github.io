Tunnels Around Campus

Have you ever wanted to get from one building to another - quicker? Or maybe you don’t want to walk outside on a bitterly cold day? Good thing Western has a modest network of tunnels to help you on your way!

Using the map below, take a look at all of the different tunnels you can use to get from place to place. Personally, I always appreciate taking the Social Science to UCC tunnel as a shortcut out of the cold.

Also, I’m going to let you in on a very rare secret, which is: how to get to my favourite tunnel. This tunnel has been perfectly (and probably accidentally) crafted to be the perfect echo chamber. You can try whispering as quiet as you can and hear it come back to you. Maybe make sure you’re alone to test it out! Here’s how to get there:

1. Enter University College (not to be confused with UCC).

2. Walk to the staircase in the very center of the building (by the main central entrance), and take it all the way to the bottom.

3. There is a door at the very bottom. This is the tunnel from University College to Lawson Hall. Enter and explore!
